Bell Pepper

Colorful, crisp, and versatile, our greenhouse-grown bell peppers are a vibrant addition to any dish. Packed with vitamins and antioxidants, they offer a sweet, mild flavor perfect for salads, stir-fries, or grilling. Grown with precision in controlled environments for year-round freshness.

Growing Conditions

  • ☀️
    Requires 6-8 hours of direct sunlight or grow lights
  • 💧
    Consistent watering, keep soil evenly moist
  • 🌡️
    Optimal temperature: 21-29°C day, 15-18°C night
  • 🌱
    pH level: 6.0-6.8 for best growth
  • 🌿
    Staking or caging recommended to support heavy fruit
